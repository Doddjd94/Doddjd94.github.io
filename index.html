Mixing Luck

12-13-2017
6:00 p.m.

This recipe was pure luck. The first time I made it I was 16. It was literally “hmmm let’s put these together and see what happens” sorta thing. Little did I know, I made a masterpiece! It’s also extremely cheap to make. So those of you who are a pasta fan be prepared to wipe up some drool... maybe, I’m new at this video stuff and kinda suck with getting the perfect image just yet. Anyways, you’ll need:

  • Knorr Creamy Pesto
  • Knorr Four Cheese
  • Knorr Parma Rosa
  • 3 3/4 cups of milk
  • 2 Tbsp of butter
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 box of Penne
Directions:
Begin with getting a pot ready for the noodles. Once boiling pour the noodles into the pot. Then, take milk, butter, olive oil, the three (3) packs of sauce mix and mix them in a medium sauce pan. Place on the stove at medium heat. Stir frequently. Once sauce begins to thicken, turn to low heat and keep stirring. The sauce should be thick and creamy when done. When the noodles are cooked, empty the water for the pot. You can add as much sauce as you want on top of your noodles.
Additional:
I also love to add grilled chicken. Simply add some salt and pepper, maybe a tad of Cajun seasoning, grill it, cut it up and add it as another topping to your meal :) I really hope you enjoy this recipe as much as my family and I do. It’s fast, simple, and of course delicious. Tell me what you think :)
