JOB POSTING

11-15-2017
5:00 p.m.

Job posting, employees needed! Have you always wanted to be your own boss? What about being the judge on how much you make at the end of the week? You could be making 6 figures a year! Sleeping in everyday in the comfort of your own bed. Having the freedom to go out and do whatever you want too. Enjoying all the time in the world with your little one(s), While literally doing nothing. Ha ha okay who am I kidding, that was all a lie. Besides getting to spend time with your babies. You know, there are always these articles on how teachers don’t make enough with everything they do; I 100% agree with that! What about us who run daycares? We handle the kids who can’t feed themselves, go to the bathroom alone, wipe their own butt, dress themselves, cant speak, can’t walk, etc... what about us? Some of us work 60+ hours a week and don’t even make enough money to put food on our table. You might say “well you don’t charge enough.” The sad truth is, to even make it in this business you have to compete with others and not by how amazing you care for kids, it’s the price. Today, most parents “price shop” for a daycare. Which is sad when you think about it. Parents don’t seem to care if their child is going to be in a clean, happy, safe, learning environment but, rather a cheap place and they are sold. Therefore, the ones who are actually out there, putting in the effort and extra money for learning supplies and activities but having to charge the amount that others who do nothing, are literally making nothing. Parents, do all of us who are trying hard to help your child grow, learn, and succeed; not only is but your child, a favor and look at what we can do, not how much we charge.
