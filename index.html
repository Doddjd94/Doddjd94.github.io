The Disadvantages of Running Your Own Daycare

10-26-2017
5:30 p.m.

What are Disadvantages of running an in home daycare?

There’s not a ton but there are a few. One of them is the fact that you can’t leave the house Monday- Friday until you close. By then your just tired and you don’t want to leave. Another is your things get broken. They are kids, they will always do stuff you’ve asked them not to do. They are also very sneaky. Kids are clumsy and they spill things. Your floor might get ruined and so can your furniture. Being careful is not one of their strong suits. So beware of paint missing off your walls, dents, boogers, and something you have no idea what it is, but it’s there and you probably want to wear gloves to get it off... if you supply any food be prepare because it gets pricey. They have a tendency to be picky so food gets wasted. You may think it’s possible at first to afford all of it. But it’s not just like feeding 1 child, your feeding 6 or more children. Who are only taking 2 bites or just mashing up the food. Foods not cheap, so I suggest if you plan to feed them, make sure you add that extra cost into your daily/weekly fee. There are some pretty rough days, especially when the kiddos are sick. Whether it’s just one kid or all of them, you will want to hide because kids scream... A LOT when they are sick. They actually just scream over everything. They could want the floor to be blue one day, but that’s not happening, so they will scream for 3 hours straight... Money is a whole other topic, just use your best judgement and be realistic. Although most people think we are crazy and say they could never do this job. Honestly there are way more good days than bad. They may be gross and destructive but they will make you laugh like you never have before.
