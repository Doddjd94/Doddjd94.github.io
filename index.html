Food Snatchers

10-20-2017

Wanna know what it’s like running a daycare? It’s called starving. Why? Because children are animals! They literally could eat a four course meal, say they are full, and that they can’t eat anymore. Then as soon as you sit down to eat every single one of them surround you like Vultures and it sounds like the seagulls off “Finding Nemo” “Mine! Mine! Mine!” As you try to shoo them away ones already sticking their hand in what your eating and before you know it, your food is either on the floor or one of them has taken off with it. So those of you who wonder how I stay skinny, here you go. I suffer from malnutrition because of these minions. Just kidding, but really as soon as the last one closes their eyes for nap, I’m no joke, high tailing it to the kitchen to scarf something down before another wakes up.
