The Porch Puddle

11-06-2017
5:00 p.m.

As a parent and a daycare provider I see some strange things as well as hear them too. It’s been raining for the passed few days kind of on and off. When it stopped raining I decided to take the kids outside. We all needed some time out of the house. As usual the kids grab their favorite outside toys and run through the yard like wild animals. But let’s talk about the animal part... everyone knows after it rains it’s usually wet outside right? Occasionally on the porch there are puddles where the woods natural dips and curves are. So these animal-like tiny humans decided to drink out of them like dogs!! I put my foot over 1 of the puddles and told them “it’s yucky!”. One of their responses was “it’s baby water!” 🤦🏻‍♀️ that was the beginning of a losing argument with a 2 year old.. unfortunately I’m not an octopus. I was unable to cover all the puddles, aka “baby water”, and the kids continued to lick the nasty porch puddles. Needless to say outside time was cut short. Like, why couldn’t they just play in the mud?
