JOB POSTING

11-15-2017
5:00 p.m.

Being a mom to a toddler is definitely exhausting. They are beginning to learn so many different things as well as understanding a whole lot more. Unfortunately, this means multiple tantrums a day. You begin hearing the word “no” more often, and when you try to get them to do something, the screaming begins. I want to give you mommy’s who are having an issue with teeth brushing, a helpful tip. At first my son beat me to the bathroom to brush his teeth and recently it’s become an awful fight. Some of the things that have help are, letting him brush my teeth while I brush his, let him use my toothbrush, and getting him a vibrating toothbrush. Those seem to really work for my little stubborn butt, I hope they help with yours.
